Liqpass

A simple compass that points to the nearest open liquor store.

How to use: Tap Find Nearest Store, hold your phone flat, and follow the red needle. Tap the store name to open it in Maps.

Store data from OpenStreetMap contributors. Liqpass shows the closest open store based on available data — always verify hours and directions independently.
